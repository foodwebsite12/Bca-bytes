About IGNOU BCA

Indira Ghandhi National Open University (IGNOU) a well-known Open University which provides BCA in both manner Online and ODL to the students.

The basic objective of the programme is to open a channel of admission for computing courses for students, who have done the 10+2 and are interested in taking computing/IT as a career.

What is BCA ?

BCA (Bachelor in Computer Application) is a 3 years graduation programme in which you have to learn about computer science. If any body failed to Join Computer Science through any college due to any reason like (failed to eligiblity creteria) he/she can enroll in BCA to pursue their learning Computer Science

What is the Eligiblity Creteria to Enroll in BCA in IGNOU ?

If you have done your class 12th through any recognised board like CBSE or any state board you can enroll in BCA programme in IGNOU the percentage you got in class 12th does not matter here in IGNOU.
